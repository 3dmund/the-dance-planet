Welcome to the Dance Planet!

    We are a mother-daughter pair that is passionate about dance. Because we know that dance has bonded us throughout these years and that there is so much to learn from the dance world, the two of us decided to launch this website, condensing all the essential information for aspiring professional dancers, recreational dancers, or any dance enthusiast.

    From the latest news in the dance world to popular dance books and movies to dance competition and company information, the Dance Planet is a helpful, accessible resource for all ages. We hope that by sharing a variety of information about what we love to do, we are spreading the appreciation of this art form that is not only culturally significant but also is a medium of expression, exercise, and connection: and we hope that you will join us at the Dance Planet on this enriching journey.
